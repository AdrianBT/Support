What is Breast cancer?

A cancer that forms in the cells of the breasts. Breast cancer can occur in women and rarely in men. Symptoms of breast cancer include a lump in the breast, bloody discharge from the nipple, and changes in the shape or texture of the nipple or breast. Treatment depends on the stage of cancer. It may consist of chemotherapy, radiation, and surgery.

Breast cancer awereness mounth!

Breast Cancer Awareness month is not to tell people that breast cancer exists, but to instead encourage people to get screened (many clinics offer free breast cancer screenings for the month) or check themselves. Breast cancer can be very treatable if caught early, and you can catch it early by just checking yourself. Most people don't do regular checks, so Breast Cancer Awareness month exists to remind and educate people.

The history of Breast cancer foundation, INC

In 1991, Janelle Hail founded the National Breast Cancer Foundation with the help of her husband and family. A breast cancer survivor herself, Janelle has made it her mission to educate other women about breast cancer and the importance of early detection.
